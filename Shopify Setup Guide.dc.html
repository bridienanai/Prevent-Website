Before you start
Have these ready: an email address for the account, your logo and product/rugby images (in the assets and uploads folders of your download), and your design files open in a browser as a visual reference. Set aside about two hours. You will not add products or take payments — this is a marketing site only.
1

Create your Shopify account

  1. Go to shopify.com and click Start free trial.
  2. Enter your email and create a password. When asked what you want to sell, you can pick “I'm still exploring” — it doesn't lock anything in.
  3. Name the store Prevent Biometrics. You can change this later in Settings.
  4. When the trial offer appears, choose the Advanced plan (the $1/month trial). You'll upgrade to Plus only when you're ready for commerce.
2

Keep it a marketing site (no shop yet)

  1. Don't add any products. With no products, there's no checkout to worry about.
  2. Hide the site from the public while you build: in the left sidebar go to Online Store → Preferences, scroll to Password protection, tick Restrict access, and set a password.
  3. You'll remove that password in the final step when you're ready to launch.
3

Choose & open your theme

  1. Go to Online Store → Themes.
  2. The free Dawn theme is already installed. For closer control of this design, click Visit Theme Store and buy Impulse or Prestige (~$300 one-time), then Add it.
  3. Next to your theme, click Customize. This opens the drag-and-drop editor where you'll build every page. Keep your design files open beside it to copy from.

4

Upload your logo & images

  1. Go to Content → Files and drag in everything from your assets and uploads folders (logos, rugby photos, product shots, press logos).
  2. Set the site logo: in the theme editor, click the header section, then Logo → Select image and pick prevent-1line-bio-white.png (white version for the dark header).
5

Build the homepage, section by section

In the editor, use Add section and match each part of the design top-to-bottom. Type the copy straight from your design files.

Hero“Image banner” — set the rugby photo as background, add the headline + two buttons.
As featured in“Logo list” — upload the five press logos.
Why it matters“Multicolumn” with 5 columns for the measurements, plus an image of the exploded mouthguard.
How it works“Image with text” — the system photo + the 3 steps.
Validation“Multicolumn” for the stats + study cards; link each card to the study URL.
Blog & Newsroom“Blog posts” section, pointed at each blog (set up in Step 7).
Support“Rich text” / “Multicolumn” with the two cards linking to your portal & help centre.
For the gradient tiles & fancy cards: the built-in sections won't reproduce them exactly. Add a “Custom Liquid” section and paste that block's code straight from the matching .dc.html file (copy from its opening <div…> to its closing </div>). It will render as-is.

6

Add your standalone pages

  1. Go to Online Store → Pages → Add page.
  2. Create a Careers page — paste the text from Careers.dc.html, and make the email a link to support@preventbiometrics.com.
  3. Add any other standalone pages the same way.
7

Set up the Blog & Newsroom

  1. Go to Online Store → Blog posts, click Manage blogs, then Add blog. Make two: one called Blog and one called Newsroom.
  2. Add your three articles to Blog (copy from the blog-*.dc.html files). For Newsroom, add a post per item — title, date, and a link out to the article.
  3. Tip: keep the “AI-assisted draft” articles in Draft until you've reviewed them.
8

Build the menu & key links

  1. Go to Online Store → Navigation → Main menu.
  2. Add menu items: Why it matters, How it works, Validation, Blog, Newsroom, Support.
  3. Add Customer Portal as a link to https://portal.preventbiometrics.com, and point Support at https://support.preventbiometrics.com (your HubSpot help centre).

9

Connect HubSpot

  1. Go to Apps (left sidebar) → Shopify App Store, search HubSpot, and click Install.
  2. Follow its prompts to connect your HubSpot account — this captures leads from any forms.
  3. Make sure your Support button and footer link point to your HubSpot help centre URL.
10

Go live

  1. Review everything in Preview. Test every menu link and button.
  2. Remove the password: Online Store → Preferences → untick Restrict access.
  3. Connect your domain: Settings → Domains → Connect existing domain, enter preventbiometrics.com, and follow the DNS steps. Tip: keep your old site running until the new one is confirmed working.
Later — when you're ready to sell
Upgrade to Shopify Plus from Settings → Plan — one click, no rebuild. Then add products, switch on checkout, and layer in subscriptions (Recharge), B2B / tiered pricing, and Buy-Now-Pay-Later. Everything you built here stays exactly as it is.
Quick gotchas
  • • Confirm the validation stats (0.97 / 0.98 CCC, 81.6% PPV) before publishing — they come from a cited study.
  • • The blog articles are AI-assisted drafts — review them first.
  • • Newsroom links point to real third-party articles; open each to confirm it still works.
  • • Save your work as you go — Shopify's editor autosaves, but click Save after big changes.
